Temple Inn & Suites

Stay closer to your heavenly home.

Here at Temple Inn & Suites, we specialize in providing hotel accommodations thoughtfully placed near the breathtaking temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. We believe that providing a place for those seeking to be near the temple is a wonderful way we can serve those around us and contribute to the Lord's work.

Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons
  • A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons
  • Long-term stay accommodations
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work

Provo, Utah

Temple Fact: The Provo City Center Temple includes art glass, finials, and other hardware that were salvaged from the remains of the Provo Tabernacle.

Tuscon, Arizona

Temple Fact: Cactus plants at the site were transplanted to an on-site nursery and reintegrated into the final landscaping.

Houston, Texas

Temple Fact: The Houston Texas Temple was the second temple built in Texas, following the Dallas Texas Temple (1984)

Baton Rouge, Louisiana

Temple Fact: The Baton Rouge Louisiana Temple was the first temple built in Louisiana.
