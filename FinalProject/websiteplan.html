    Site Purpose

    The purpose of this site is to provide up-to-date forecasts on the climate, weather, and water for the communities and businesses of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho.
    With Food Production, Outdoor Recreation, Tourism, and Energy being four of the key industries in Idaho,
    providing up-to-date information in regard to the air quality levels, water quality, and temperatures are essential in ensuring the best quality for Idaho citizens and commerce.
