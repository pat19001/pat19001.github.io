    • Site Name: Temple Inn & Suites

    Site Purpose

    Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain that provides lodging accommodations near the Temples of the Church of Jesus Christ. Specifically, the chain was created to provide lodging and other services to temple patrons, workers, their families, and those who are seeking to attend the temple for various occasions. The overall objective for the website is to promote and provide readily available information about the services and accomodations that Temple Inn & Suites offers to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.

    Target Audience

    The target audience for this website is moreso tailored to members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints but encompasses all who are interested in temples. Temple Inn & Suites provides lodging accommodations for individuals who are staying for an extended period of time or those who are visiting for events such as weddings, receptions, sealings, or temple trips. Visitors of this website might be friends and family of relatives who are serving, attending, or being sealed in a temple. The website will provide visitors with room sizes, bedding accommodations, amenities, pricing, availability, and nearby attractions and services.
